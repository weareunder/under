but why?

yes. in just 10 days, we develop a clear vision, identify key values, and create a fresh brand identity. really.

quick

the sprint's essence lies in efficiency, not necessarily speed. the process is scientific and straightforward, with decision-making occurring within a short timeframe. it's almost like doing yourself a favor: suffer shortly, enjoy longer.

fancy

this process is a dance - design. test it. repeat. we found ways to ensure that working fast didn't mean sacrificing the quality of our work. this balance allowed us to turn challenges into wins.

team

the brand sprint team includes inbal and eden as creative directors. additionally, the core team includes a wide range of skills, from 2d/3d design, illustration, and motion to other disciplines like development and ai. for everything else, we source talent worldwide from our pool of talent, which gives us great flexibility.

10-day plan

creating a brand that sticks can be like solving a big puzzle. our 10-day plan method is like a map to help you find your way. it's all about starting big, then getting more and more focused until you find that perfect fit for your brand.

day 0 strategy

let’s go! we start with in-depth research by using the three hours brand sprint, a streamlined session for turning thousands of abstract ideas you have about “your brand” into something more concrete. We'll talk about your past, present, and future. we'll ask why, how, and what. we'll discuss values and outline your audiences and competition.

day 1-3 research

let's stretch it out! we gain deeper insights into your brand strategy, point of view, approach, and tone of voice. We'll write the brand story and use it as our pillar while exploring potential concepts. We collect visuals—images, textures, typography, and colors—to portray a different brand experience and commit to one direction we all agree on.

day 4-6 design

one clear creative visual direction! go! we'll develop a visual language, including logos, typography, color palettes, visual assets, and more. we’ll explore how it stretches through all necessary mediums of communication.

day 9 present

we will present your entire visual identity, review the whole brand, and ensure that it is greater than the sum of its parts.

day 10 purify

we'll refine your design direction to a final visual identity.

day 11 handoff

you have a visual identity. let's “pack it up” so you and your team can make the best use of it and scale it.

handshake

we'll meet to appreciate the work we've accomplished and continue to support anything related to the brand moving forward.
