A 10-day brand sprint for Grain

When SMBs deal with multi-currency transactions across borders, their finances become increasingly complex. To provide business certainty to larger companies, banks provide hedging services. Grain believes SMBs should access affordable services tailored to their financial needs on a simple, seamless, and accessible platform.
