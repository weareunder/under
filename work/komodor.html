Komodor

A Brand sprint for Komodor, a platform that helps DevOps troubleshoot efficiently. Komodor tracks changes across your complete K8s-based stack understands their ripple effect and gives dev & ops teams the context they need to troubleshoot efficiently. Independently.

In 10 days, we created a full visual identity, from brand strategy & positioning to brand guidelines, logo & sub-logos, icons, and a complete assets library.
