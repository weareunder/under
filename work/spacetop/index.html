A 10-day Brand sprint for Spacetop

Brand Story (by Andy Bellass)
This is a story all about one thing. One thing that is one thing that is our goal. That is our biggest asset. That is what we must own. That is the heart of everything that is what we have lost. That is what we need to regain. That is what we so desperately need. That is what we must own that we have created. That is the heart of success. That is where productivity grows. That is where creativity lives and breathes. That is a place and not a place. That is our space. The space in our cafe. The space on a plane. The space on my sofa. The space I feel sane. The space for my teams. The space to collaborate, The space to work, meditate. The space to pontificate. The limitless space. The free space. The dream space. The space that grows. The space that flows, your space. Our space, my creative space, my workspace, my infinite workspace, my giant space in the smallest place. My go-deep space.
My research space, my play space, my imagination space, my quiet space, my flow space, my space, my space top.
Re-think your space, re-find your space, re-imagine your space own your own space, own your own space in any space with space top.
So, we're not selling a new kind of laptop.
What we're doing, what our job to do is to own, define, create and inspire people into a new form of space.
So ultimately, we're selling space, we're selling the new space.
