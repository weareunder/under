A 10-day brand sprint for PointFive

In this fast digital world, companies struggle with controlling cloud costs. The economy calls for cost-cutting and efficiency, forcing finance, management, and engineering teams to work tirelessly, constantly monitoring and adjusting infrastructures to prevent overspending. At the same time, cloud giants keep adding more capabilities, making it very hard to catch up. There are tools to see cloud costs. Yet, it’s time to raise the bar and close the gap between knowing and doing. It’s time for businesses to shake off the weight of cloud costs and grow more efficiently. It’s time to move to autopilot! PointFive is designed to pinpoint inefficiencies and turn them into savings. It connects the opportunity to its relevant resources and engineers, sends practical and actionable alerts, and fixes things by itself! Our purpose is to help businesses gain financial health by boosting efficiency, cutting costs, and unlocking the power of their complex cloud systems. With PointFive, companies can grow, be sustainable, and thrive. Let’s make it happen!
